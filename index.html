Vidya Balan

Vidya Balan is an indian actress known for her versatile performances in Bollywood films.

Career

Vidya began her career in television and gained recognition with her debut film "Parineeta" in 2005. She is known for breaking stereotypes with roles in movies like "Kahaani", "The Dirty Picture", and "Tumhari Sulu".

Awards

Vidya Balan has received a National Film Award and several Filmfare Awards, establishing herself as one of the finest actors of her generation.
